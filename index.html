Basic Rules

  • Respect all players and staff.
  • No RDM, VDM, or FailRP.
  • No exploiting, cheating, or modding.
  • Keep RP realistic and story‑driven.
  • Follow staff instructions at all times.

Basic New Player Guide

  • Join the Discord and read the rules.
  • Make a realistic character with a backstory.
  • Learn the city layout and job locations.
  • Use /report for help or issues.
  • Respect the RP of others and build stories.
